# A Minimal Paper (paper-latex example)

## Abstract
This sample shows the paper-latex adapter compiling a Markdown draft to a LaTeX article and PDF,
including a table and a Unicode symbol (rel_err ≤ 5%).

## Results

| config | pred | meas | rel_err |
|---|---|---|---|
| A | 100 | 102 | 1.96% |
| B | 200 | 199 | 0.50% |

The model attains ≤2% error across configs.
