# Survey Five Source Classes

Status: accepted

Every Kaoju work survey shall look for papers, technical reports, source code repositories, datasets, and models. The survey records coverage or absence for each class so that a literature result cannot silently stand in for implementation, data, or model evidence.

## Consequences

- ADR 0001 still governs presentation: papers and technical reports are primary Related-Work Catalog entries, while repositories, datasets, and models are linked implementation or evidence artifacts when a reliable relationship exists.
- Search and coverage records must distinguish the five source classes and report when a class yields no eligible or accessible result.
- Benchmark specifications, issues, release notes, and other useful sources may supplement the survey but do not replace the five required classes.
