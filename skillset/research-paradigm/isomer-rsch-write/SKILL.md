Use this skill when evidence is ready for report or manuscript drafting,
revision, checkpointing, review packaging, or finalization routing.

Read first:

- `../isomer-rsch-shared/SKILL.md`
- Source analysis: `../../../context/explore/deepscientist-skill-analysis/write.md`

## Entry Signals

- Durable evidence is ready for report, manuscript, checkpoint, review package,
  or finalization drafting.
- An outline, evidence ledger, experiment matrix, references, or figure status
  can be refreshed.
- The task needs prose that stays inside supported evidence boundaries.

## Exit Criteria

- Updated sections, draft package, or review package are durable.
- Claims, references, figures, metrics, and method details are aligned with
  recorded evidence.
- Any route-back to analysis, review, finalize, or blocker is recorded.

## Procedure

1. Refresh control state, outline, evidence ledger, experiment matrix,
   references, figures, and bundle status.
2. Repair the outline before drafting if claim scope or evidence boundaries are
   weak.
3. Separate claims, settings, reproducibility details, implementation details,
   and provenance.
4. Refresh citations from real sources.
5. Plan displays before prose; use paper-plot for first-pass standard figures
   and figure-polish for final figure QA.
6. Draft section jobs only inside supported evidence boundaries.
7. Validate coverage, language, claims, figures, and bundle readiness.
8. Route to analysis, review, finalize, or blocker.

## Durable Outputs

- Updated paper or report sections.
- Aligned evidence ledger, experiment matrix, references, and figure status.
- Draft, review, or submission package Artifact.
- Route-back Decision Record when evidence or outline is insufficient.

## Guardrails

- Do not write citations, metrics, or method details from recollection.
- Do not use polished prose to hide missing evidence.
- Do not put agent-control language into manuscript text.
- Use `[[tbd-surface:path-paper-layout]]` for unsettled paper layouts.
