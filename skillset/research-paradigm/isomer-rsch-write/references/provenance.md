# Provenance

This skill adapts the DeepScientist `write` research method and supporting writing references into a self-contained Isomer Labs skill.

The adaptation preserves reusable methodology: writing contract refresh, pre-write revision strategy, claim-evidence discipline, citation truth, manuscript cleanliness, oral-package upgrade, section-level rewriting, experiments and analysis writing, display-role planning, publishability stop-loss, and bundle routing.

Source-runtime concepts were intentionally translated:

- lifecycle-level research work became Research Inquiry, Research Task, Research Inquiry Relationship, Run, and Workflow Stage Cursor language.
- artifact operations became Artifacts, Evidence Items, Decision Records, Gates, Provenance Records, or host Artifact APIs.
- memory operations became Findings, Evidence Items, Artifacts, or durable context queries.
- command execution became Execution Adapter Command Request with Capability Binding refs.
- paper paths and generated layouts became Topic Workspace, paper Artifacts or semantic Artifact scopes resolved through Workspace Path Resolution when ordinary locations are needed; implementation-specific APIs, schemas, providers, and policies remain behind accepted extension refs.
- citation providers became Literature Provider Binding refs.

Venue LaTeX templates were not imported in this slice. See `deferred-venue-templates.md` for the decision and follow-up criteria.
