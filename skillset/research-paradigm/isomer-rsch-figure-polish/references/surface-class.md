# Surface Class

Classify the figure before polishing because export expectations, review strictness, and handoff records differ by target surface.

| Surface | Use When | Export Expectation | Review Strictness |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `milestone` | The figure summarizes a result for an Operator Agent update, project checkpoint, or durable progress report. | PNG preview is usually enough unless the report requires vector output. | Message must be obvious quickly and source evidence must be linked. |
| `paper_main` | The figure may appear as a main manuscript figure. | Vector export such as PDF or SVG plus PNG preview. | Highest strictness; readable at likely single-column or double-column size. |
| `appendix` | The figure supports supplementary or appendix detail. | Vector plus preview when possible. | Can carry more detail, but must avoid dashboard clutter. |
| `review_response` | The figure supports reviewer response, rebuttal, or revision evidence. | Match response package needs and keep preview convenient. | Link to reviewer item and evidence update. |
| `internal_review` | The figure helps diagnose, compare, or decide internally. | PNG or host-preferred preview may be enough. | Still inspect if it may later be promoted. |

## Surface Questions

- Who will read the figure first: Operator Agent, reviewer, paper reader, internal team, or future agent?
- Is the figure evidence-facing, claim-facing, or only diagnostic?
- Will it be down-scaled, embedded in a report, or cited by a Research Claim?
- Does Gate Policy preflight require a resolved Gate before cost, privacy, export, publication-facing output, or external upload?
