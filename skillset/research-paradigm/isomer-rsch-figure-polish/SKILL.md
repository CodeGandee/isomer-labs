Use this skill when an already meaningful figure needs render-inspect-revise
quality control, final exports, claim linkage, or paper-facing polish.

Read first:

- `../isomer-rsch-shared/SKILL.md`
- Source analysis: `../../../context/explore/deepscientist-skill-analysis/figure-polish.md`

## Entry Signals

- An already meaningful figure needs render-inspect-revise quality control.
- The figure has a known surface: milestone, manuscript, appendix, review, or
  internal.
- The comparison or claim the figure must communicate can be stated.

## Exit Criteria

- Final figure exports have been rendered and inspected.
- The source plotting Artifact or generation path is linked.
- Claim, Evidence Item, report, or review linkage is explicit.

## Procedure

1. Classify the surface: milestone, manuscript main figure, appendix figure,
   review figure, or internal figure.
2. Define the one comparison or claim the figure must communicate.
3. Choose the chart form that matches the research issue.
4. Apply restrained academic styling: white background, muted palette,
   minimal legend, clear hierarchy, and no decoration.
5. Render the figure and inspect the actual image output.
6. Revise readability, labels, clutter, composition, and export settings.
7. Export final durable formats and link the figure to supporting evidence.

## Durable Outputs

- Final figure exports.
- Source plotting Artifact or generation path.
- Claim, Evidence Item, or report linkage.

## Guardrails

- Do not treat uninspected renders as final.
- Do not polish disposable debug plots unless explicitly instructed.
- Do not overload one figure with unrelated claims.
- Use `[[tbd-surface:schema-figure-output]]` when final export schema is unsettled.
