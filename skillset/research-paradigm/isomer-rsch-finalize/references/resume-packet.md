# Resume Packet

Use this reference when the Research Thread is pausing, parking, publishing with follow-up, or otherwise needs a compact restart path.

## Recommended Structure

### 1. Current State

- Research Goal:
- present Workflow Stage:
- final recommendation at pause time:
- closure state: stop | park | publish | archive | continue later | new loop

### 2. Current Node History

- current active node:
- predecessor nodes:
- superseded nodes or closure paths:
- why the current node is the authoritative resume point:

### 3. Accepted Baseline

- baseline id or Artifact:
- reproducibility or comparability status:
- key metric context:

### 4. Strongest Evidence

- top two or three Evidence Items:
- supporting Artifacts:
- caveats:

### 5. Open Blockers

- blocker:
- why it matters:
- stage that should resolve it:

### 6. Next Action

- single best next step:
- why it dominates alternatives:
- what should be read first:

### 7. Do-Not-Repeat Notes

- failed branches not worth retrying unchanged:
- misleading metrics or comparability traps:
- known environment, execution, or tooling pitfalls:

### 8. Reopen Conditions

- exact condition that would justify reopening:
- Gate or Operator Agent choice needed:
