# Finalization Checklist

Use this checklist before closing, pausing, archiving, publishing, or handing off a Research Thread or Research Task.

## Core Closure Questions

Confirm what is genuinely supported, what is only partially supported, what failed or was abandoned, what remains unresolved, what the recommended next action is, and what would justify reopening.

## Closure Preconditions

- accepted baseline or explicit baseline waiver:
- accepted Runs and analysis state:
- writing, report, or paper state:
- review, proofing, or submission state when relevant:
- blockers and open Gates:
- latest Decision Records:
- package inventory:
- final claim ledger:
- resume or handoff need:

## Final Report Contents

A strong final report usually includes executive state, strongest supported findings, negative results worth preserving, limitations, package or bundle status, recommendation, reopen conditions, and first-read sources for resumption.

## Anti-Patterns

Do not finalize by erasing negative evidence, calling the task successful without claim statuses, omitting package inventory, relying on conversation memory, or saying "done" without a Decision Record and closure rationale.

## Route-Back Conditions

Route through decision when closure reveals unresolved major claims, unresolved writing gates, missing proofing or submission checks, unclear recommendation, missing handoff packet, stale summary state, or unresolved package inventory.
