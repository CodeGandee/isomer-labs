# Controlled Brainstorming

Use this reference when the current route is not obvious and the idea stage needs a real divergent pass. The goal is a bounded, differentiated slate that is broad enough to avoid premature convergence and narrow enough to audit.

## Entry Condition

Do not brainstorm until an objective contract and current-board packet already exist. If either is fuzzy, fix framing first.

## Family Mix

Decide which route families are allowed:

- `mechanism_family`: new algorithmic or modeling idea.
- `objective_family`: change the training target, ranking target, or optimization target.
- `measurement_family`: change the evaluator, validation lens, or trusted observable.
- `infrastructure_family`: change throughput, batching, staging, or system constraints that affect useful iteration.

Do not default to mechanism-family routes only.

## Bounded Slate

Default target: six to twelve raw ideas, collapsed to two or three serious candidates and at most five.

Require visible differentiation: one local refinement of the incumbent, one orthogonal alternative, one objective or measurement route when that layer may be wrong, and one infrastructure or iteration-speed route when throughput itself is blocking progress.

## Filters

Discard or downgrade candidates that only improve a surrogate, violate hard constraints, are micro-variants, reopen stale routes without new evidence, or lack a cheap falsification path.

## Serious Candidate Fields

- family type
- targeted limitation
- why now
- strongest prior-work overlap
- anti-win condition
- minimal validation
- abandonment condition

## Exit Rule

The selected route should beat alternatives on evidence-per-run and decision value, not just novelty.
