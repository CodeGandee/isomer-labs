# V2 Semantic Placeholders

This registry defines the meaning of v2 research objects before Isomer Labs binds them to storage. Every placeholder below is semantic only. It is not yet bound to Artifact, Evidence Item, Run, Gate, Decision Record, Provenance Record, path, storage label, or database schema.

| ID | Meaning | Required semantic content | Typical producers | Typical consumers | Storage-binding status |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `workspace-bootstrap` | The post-specialization v2 research workspace readiness contract. | Topic refs, profile bundle readiness, runtime readiness, semantic surface plan, placeholder binding registry, Agent Workspace access posture, validation status, and blockers. | `isomer-rsch-workspace-mgr-v2` | All v2 research skills, Topic Service Master, Project Operator Session, and Operator Agent | Not storage-bound yet. |
| `research-frame` | The scoped research problem and success boundary. | Problem statement, objective, constraints, metric or evaluation need, known unknowns, and next likely stage. | `isomer-rsch-scout-v2`, `isomer-rsch-decision-v2` | `isomer-rsch-baseline-v2`, `isomer-rsch-idea-v2`, `isomer-rsch-finalize-v2` | Not storage-bound yet. |
| `comparator-contract` | The comparator and measurement basis that make later progress meaningful. | Comparator identity or waiver reason, metric definition, comparability assumptions, accepted deviations, and blockers. | `isomer-rsch-baseline-v2`, `isomer-rsch-decision-v2` | `isomer-rsch-idea-v2`, `isomer-rsch-experiment-v2`, `isomer-rsch-analysis-v2` | Not storage-bound yet. |
| `selected-hypothesis` | One falsifiable route chosen from the current research situation. | Hypothesis, expected effect, mechanism sketch, novelty boundary, risk, falsification condition, and next experiment. | `isomer-rsch-idea-v2`, `isomer-rsch-optimize-v2`, `isomer-rsch-decision-v2` | `isomer-rsch-experiment-v2`, `isomer-rsch-optimize-v2`, `isomer-rsch-analysis-v2` | Not storage-bound yet. |
| `optimization-frontier` | The candidate set and ranking logic for algorithm-first search. | Candidate briefs, ranking criteria, current best line, rejected or parked ideas, fusion/debug notes, and next selected route. | `isomer-rsch-optimize-v2` | `isomer-rsch-idea-v2`, `isomer-rsch-experiment-v2`, `isomer-rsch-decision-v2` | Not storage-bound yet. |
| `experiment-contract` | The bounded plan that makes one test interpretable. | Selected hypothesis, comparator basis, metric, input conditions, command or procedure summary, expected outputs, and stop condition. | `isomer-rsch-experiment-v2`, `isomer-rsch-science-v2` | `isomer-rsch-experiment-v2`, `isomer-rsch-analysis-v2`, `isomer-rsch-decision-v2` | Not storage-bound yet. |
| `experiment-result` | The measured outcome of one bounded test. | What was run, observed metrics, important outputs, failures or anomalies, comparator relation, and interpretation. | `isomer-rsch-experiment-v2`, `isomer-rsch-science-v2` | `isomer-rsch-analysis-v2`, `isomer-rsch-decision-v2`, `isomer-rsch-finalize-v2` | Not storage-bound yet. |
| `analysis-finding` | A focused interpretation that changes or tests a result or claim. | Parent result, question answered, slice or check performed, finding, caveat, claim impact, and next route. | `isomer-rsch-analysis-v2`, `isomer-rsch-science-v2` | `isomer-rsch-decision-v2`, `isomer-rsch-finalize-v2`, `isomer-rsch-idea-v2` | Not storage-bound yet. |
| `science-validity-note` | A computation, package, data, simulation, or validation note that affects trust. | Scientific task, inputs, assumptions, tool or method summary, checks performed, validity verdict, and limits. | `isomer-rsch-science-v2` | `isomer-rsch-experiment-v2`, `isomer-rsch-analysis-v2`, `isomer-rsch-decision-v2` | Not storage-bound yet. |
| `route-decision` | A justified research routing choice. | Decision question, considered options, chosen route, evidence basis, rejected alternatives, blocker if any, and next action. | `isomer-rsch-decision-v2`, any v2 skill when the route is obvious | Any v2 skill | Not storage-bound yet. |
| `final-summary` | The responsible end-state summary for pause, closure, handoff, or publication-facing work. | Final position, supported claims, limitations, unresolved risks, useful outputs, recommended next action, and stop or handoff reason. | `isomer-rsch-finalize-v2` | Maintainers, downstream agents, future write/review skills | Not storage-bound yet. |
