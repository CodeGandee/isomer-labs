# Provenance

This skill adapts the DeepScientist `decision` research method and reference material into a self-contained Isomer Labs skill.

The adaptation preserves reusable methodology: decision-readiness checks, one-route judgment, canonical action labels, research-route criteria, user-input gates, baseline-reuse care, paper-route stop-loss rules, algorithm-first frontier routing, checkpoint resume packets, and decision-quality checks.

Source-runtime concepts were intentionally translated:

- quest lifecycle language became Research Thread, Research Task, Research Branch, Run, and Workflow Stage language.
- artifact operations became Artifacts, Evidence Items, Decision Records, Gates, Provenance Records, or host Artifact APIs.
- memory operations became Findings, Evidence Items, Artifacts, or durable context queries.
- command, git, and environment inspection became Capability Binding through an Execution Adapter.
- branch and paper-route runtime actions became Decision Records, Gate recommendations, Research Branch decisions, Artifacts, or handoffs.
- scheduler and continuation terms became Workflow Stage recommendations, Gates, Decision Records, observations, or pauses for Operator Agent instruction.
