# Provenance

The research-paradigm skills are derived from local method analysis notes that summarized DeepScientist source skill behavior.

The v1 generation under `v1/` preserves the first Isomer Labs adaptation of those DeepScientist-derived skills. It keeps the richer stage guidance, paper-facing companions, storage vocabulary, lifecycle vocabulary, provenance context, and license notices with generation-suffixed skill names.

The v2 generation under `v2/` distills the core research-conduction process from `context/explore/deepscientist-skill-analysis/`. V2 keeps the research loop and guardrails, then replaces storage-facing outputs with semantic placeholders so their meaning can be settled before storage binding.
