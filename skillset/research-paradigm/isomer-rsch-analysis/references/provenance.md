# Provenance

This skill adapts the DeepScientist `analysis-campaign` research method and
reference material into a self-contained Isomer Labs skill.

The adaptation preserves reusable methodology: campaign route selection,
slice-evidence contracts, comparability boundaries, writing-facing evidence,
boundary cases, durable record templates, gate checklists, and operational
guidance.

Source-runtime concepts were intentionally translated:

- quest lifecycle language became Research Thread, Research Task, Research
  Branch, Run, and Workflow Stage language.
- artifact operations became Artifacts, Evidence Items, Decision Records,
  Gates, Provenance Records, or host Artifact APIs.
- memory operations became Findings, Evidence Items, Artifacts, or durable
  context queries.
- command execution became Capability Binding through an Execution Adapter.
- source worktree and workspace assumptions became Isomer Workspace,
  Workspace Runtime, Agent Workspace, or TBD placeholders.
- scheduler and continuation terms became Workflow Stage recommendations,
  Gates, Decision Records, observations, or pauses for Operator Agent
  instruction.
