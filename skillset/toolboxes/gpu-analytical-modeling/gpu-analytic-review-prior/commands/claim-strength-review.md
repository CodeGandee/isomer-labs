# Claim Strength Review

Before review output is accepted, classify central runtime, throughput, counter-trend, saturated-component, blocking-path, model-generality, and implementation-readiness claims as supported, partially supported, unsupported, or deferred.

Supported central claims need visible evidence packets: relevant inputs, predictions, measured latency when latency is claimed, observed bottleneck evidence when component or path is claimed, mapping rationale, and match or mismatch interpretation.

If a claim was weakened, preserve why it was weakened so later stages do not restore stronger wording by accident.
