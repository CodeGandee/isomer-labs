# Mismatch Route

When GPU model evidence is incomplete or contradictory, classify the smallest mismatch class: harness issue, counter mapping error, missing hardware node, hidden overlap, cache or locality effect, scheduler or occupancy effect, unsupported shape or precision regime, invalid calibration parameter, proxy evidence not transferring, missing measured latency, or missing bottleneck observation.

Route to refine, add evidence, limit the claim, park, block, or accept partial support. Do not fit a new coefficient before checking harness, measurement context, and counter mapping.

Record the mismatch, likely cause, rejected causes, evidence gap, selected route, and reopen condition.
